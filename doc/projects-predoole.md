## KADAC.ai — Vendor & S2P Module (Apr 2026 – Present)

See full product context in [kadac-product.md](kadac-product.md)

**KADAC.ai** is Predoole's AI-powered finance operations platform for CFOs and finance teams. Ajay joined the product in April 2026 to drive AI-powered improvements in the Vendor & Source-to-Pay (S2P) module.

### Active Feature — Vendor Enrollment with OCR

**Problem:** Vendors submit ID cards and compliance documents manually. Staff re-enter the data by hand — slow, error-prone, and unscalable.

**Solution being built:** OCR-powered document intelligence pipeline that:
- Scans submitted ID card images and documents
- Extracts structured data (name, ID number, address, expiry, etc.) automatically
- Auto-populates vendor enrollment forms — zero manual re-entry
- Integrates with S2P workflow (approval routing, validation, DPDP compliance)

**Impact:** Converts a long manual workflow into a near-instant automated process — consistent with KADAC's platform goal of 80% faster AP processing.

**Tech:** OCR, AI document processing, data extraction pipeline, S2P workflow integration
